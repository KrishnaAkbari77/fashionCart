FAQs

WHERE IS MY REFUND? +

Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after we receive your returned item. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is initiated.

HOW DO I CHANGE MY ORDER? +

To change your order, please contact our Customer Service Team as soon as possible. Changes can only be made before the order is shipped.

I WANT TO RETURN SOMETHING +

Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery. Products must be unused, unwashed, and with original tags intact.
